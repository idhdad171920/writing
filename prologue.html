From the echo of that scream, thirteen silhouettes formed, sculpted from fire, gravity, and the raw bones of existence. Their eyes opened like newborn suns. They watched the Second Secret: where life blooms, suffering follows. Worlds birthing war. Civilizations birthing greed. Joy birthing mourning. Hope birthing grief. And then the Third Secret: only nothingness is mercy. To return all things to silence would end suffering forever.

With dying sight, they searched the cosmos for a species capable of carrying their legacy. They searched across the cosmos and found worlds of living flame, worlds carved from crystal, worlds older than galaxies, worlds advanced beyond imagination, worlds primitive as dust.
